  • Electronics

    The Audio System includes AM/FM/CD, Aux Ports with USB connectors, as well as Steering Wheel Audio Controls. An available touchscreen LCD Screen is home to your Entertainment system which allows you to control your iPod or tune into your favourite satellite radio station.

  • Versatility

    The well equipped interior is designed to allow you to transport friends or gear. Don't let the small size deceive you, as the iQ can comfortably seat three adults plus their luggage. There's also a storage space underneath the seats to store valuables out of sight.

  • Maneuverability

    With its short wheelbase and compact overall length—a mere 305 cm—iQ moves nimbly through the urban landscape and easily parks where others can't even consider.
